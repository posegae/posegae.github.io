About me

Hello! I'm a senior Computer Science major at Carleton College in Northfield, Minnesota.

I grew up in St. Louis, Missouri, and first came to Carleton with the intent of double-majoring in math and English. Then I took Intro CS and was hooked. Now I'm interested in creating inclusive and accessible technologies, combining problem-solving and empathy to make a positive difference in people's everyday lives. I have professional experience in app development, software design, and have done research involving both machine learning and jellyfish.
